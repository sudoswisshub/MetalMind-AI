Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. LocalAI runs 100% on your device. Your conversations never leave your phone, and we do not collect any personal data.

How do I reset the AI personality?

Go to Settings > AI Personality and tap the "Reset Default" button to restore the original system prompt.

Why is the AI sometimes slow?

LocalAI uses the processing power of your device. On older devices or when running complex models, generation might take a bit longer. Ensuring your device is charged and not in Low Power Mode can help.
