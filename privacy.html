Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

At LocalAI, we believe that your conversations with AI should remain private. Your data never leaves your device.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, conversation history, or usage analytics to our servers. All processing happens locally on your device using on-device machine learning models.

2. On-Device Processing

LocalAI uses Apple Intelligence and other local LLM engines to process your inputs. No text, voice, or image data is sent to the cloud for inference.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services. Your app usage data remains on your device and is only accessible to you.

4. Data Retention

Your chat history is stored locally on your device. You have full control over your data and can delete your history at any time within the app settings.

5. Contact Us
