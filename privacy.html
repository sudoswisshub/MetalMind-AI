Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

At Own Ai, your privacy is not a featureâ€”it is our foundation. We believe that your conversations with AI are personal and should remain that way. Most processing happens on-device, and when you choose Apple Intelligence, your content may be processed by Apple to generate responses.

1. Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, conversation history, or usage analytics to any remote servers. If you choose Apple Intelligence, Apple may process your prompts and any documents you choose to analyze to generate responses. We do not receive or store that content on our servers.

2. On-Device Processing

AI inference and speech processing primarily occur locally on your device hardware, with Apple Intelligence able to use Apple Private Cloud Compute to generate responses when selected.

  • Apple Intelligence & Speech: Speech-to-text and text-to-speech are performed on-device using Apple's Speech Framework. Apple Intelligence responses may be processed by Apple (including Apple Private Cloud Compute) to generate outputs.
  • Local LLMs (MLX/Gemma): Models are downloaded from public repositories (e.g. Hugging Face) and stored locally. Inference runs directly on your device's Silicon (GPU/Neural Engine). Downloading models uses network access but does not transmit your prompts or documents.
When using local models, no text, voice recordings, or document contents are sent to the cloud for processing.

3. Document Privacy (RAG)

When you upload documents for analysis, they are processed and indexed locally. The extracted text and vector embeddings remain strictly within your device's secure storage. If you choose Apple Intelligence, document content may be processed by Apple to generate responses.

4. Third-Party Services

Own Ai does not include third-party tracking, advertising, or analytics SDKs. Apple Intelligence is provided by Apple and may process content when selected. Local models are downloaded from public model repositories, but your prompts and documents are not sent to those services.

5. Data Retention & Control

Your chat history is saved only in your device's local database. You can clear individual conversations or reset the entire app history at any time through the settings menu. Once deleted, this data is gone and cannot be recovered by us.
